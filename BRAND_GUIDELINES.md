# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Specifications

### Color Palette
- Soft Beige: #E8DCC8
- Sage Green: #9CA89A
- Dusty Blue: #8B9BA8
- Warm Grey: #A89F97

### Typography Guidelines
- **Primary Font:** Montserrat, sans-serif
- **Secondary Font:** Lora, serif
- **Font Sizes:**
  - Small: 12px
  - Base: 16px
  - Large: 20px
  - H1: 32px
  - H2: 28px
  - H3: 24px

### Spacing Rules
- **Margins:** 16px
- **Padding:** 12px

### Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Friendly, sophisticated, engaging
- **Voice:** Conversational yet informative
